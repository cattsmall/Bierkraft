Beer, Cheese, Chocolate, Charcuterie and More.

Our stock of more than 1,000 different beers rotates seasonally. With a large selection of beer in combination with a varied selection of food and snacks, Bierkraft is the destination for beer drinkers and food enthusiasts alike.

Bierkraft is located in Park Slope, Brooklyn. We pride ourselves in our ability to bring high-quality beer, cheeses, and meats from around the globe to you.

Every weekend we offer a sandwich special that showcases seasonal ingredients and the creativity of the kitchen staff. And if you’re not in the mood to sit down and drink a beer, you can always wash down your meal with one of our many non-alcoholic beverages.

For those with a sweet tooth, we offer various chocolates, cookies, brownies and ice cream sandwiches, all of which were made at least in New York, if not in-house.

We’re also proud to host weekly beer tastings on Tuesdays at 7pm, and draught and cask takeovers by local breweries. We aim to offer a variety of beers that aren’t available in bottles, and we encourage those with an adventurous palate to try beers that they may not be able to find in most establishments.
